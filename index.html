About me

Hello! I am Oana, an artist with a passion for technology based in Cluj-Napoca, Romania.
I am passionate about UX Design and Web Development (especially front-end technologies) and I work daily to improve my knowledge in this field.
I enjoy learning how human beings are able to interact with certain applications or websites. I am taking my time to always identify the problem and than to find the simplest solution possible for the users to be able to achieve their end goal.
I focus on learning and creating unique and attractive digital products, such as memorable, user-friendly websites.
I will not stick around on just doing UI/UX & Graphic Design projects. My goal is to take it to the next level of becoming a designer and become one of the world's top designers, so now, I am working towards it. I have prepared myself to accept the challenges of this ever-changing world and I will try my very best to achieve my dreams and goals.
My goal is to become a Full Stack Designer!
