Laravel Framework

Web Application in order to promote tourism in Jiu Valley.

Starting from personal motivation to develop the tourism industry in the Jiu Valley (birth place), I have developed a web platform to promote tourism in this region of the country.
My mission was to develop a specialized web platform in which potential tourists create their first impression of the tourist destination and access information about the area. Also, users are offered the possibility to upload and share their own experiences within the platform, following their registration in the database. I developed this application following an online tutorial, the documentation related to Laravel Framework and other sources available on the Internet.
